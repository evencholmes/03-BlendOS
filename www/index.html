About BlendOS

BlendOS v5.0 — Blender Skill Tracker

Just a pet project to track Blender progress. Nothing more, nothing less. 100+ skills across 11 disciplines, a 🎯 Aim system to highlight the path to any goal, shortcuts trove, progress heatmap, and streak tracking.

All data lives on your device only. Nothing is ever sent to a server. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Export a .blendos backup regularly just in case.
